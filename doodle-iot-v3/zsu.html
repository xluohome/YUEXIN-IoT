Why Choose ZSU?

📶

+20 dBm TX Power — Long Range

With a maximum +20 dBm transmit power, the ZSU provides significantly longer range than standard Zigbee modules — reducing the number of routers needed in a mesh network and extending coverage to large homes and commercial buildings.

🔒

Hardware AES 128/256 Encryption

Built-in AES hardware encryption (AES 128/256) provides robust security for industrial and commercial IoT applications without consuming CPU resources.

💾

1MB Flash / 96KB RAM — Most Memory

The ZSU has the highest memory capacity in our Zigbee lineup: 1MB flash and 96KB RAM enable complex firmware, large application code, and extensive data logging without external storage.

🌡️

Industrial Temperature Range

-40°C to 105°C operating range makes the ZSU suitable for demanding environments including industrial control, outdoor installations, and temperature-extreme applications.
