Why Choose ZS3L?

📶

Zigbee 3.0 enables self-healing mesh networking — devices can relay signals beyond Wi-Fi range, ideal for large homes and commercial buildings.

⚡

DSP + FPU for Signal Processing

ARM Cortex-M33 with DSP instructions and FPU enables on-module signal processing — useful for sensor data filtering and control algorithms.

Wide Voltage Range

2.0V–3.8V operating range provides flexibility for both 3.3V and 2×AA battery-powered designs without additional regulators.
