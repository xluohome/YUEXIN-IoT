Why Choose ZTU?

🔌

Maximum GPIO Count

15 GPIO pins plus 2 ADC channels provide the highest I/O density in our Zigbee lineup — ideal for complex multi-sensor designs and multi-relay control panels.

📶

IPEX Antenna Option

ZTU-IPEX variant supports an external IPEX antenna connector, enabling use of higher-gain antennas or remote antenna placement for better RF performance.

🔋

Low Voltage Operation

1.8V minimum operating voltage enables direct battery operation with 2×AA or single-cell LiPo without voltage regulation — ideal for wireless sensor nodes.

🔄

Dual Production Modes

Supports both SMT and wave soldering for maximum manufacturing flexibility across different production volumes and assembly lines.
