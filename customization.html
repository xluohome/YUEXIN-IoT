Module Customization Service

Adapt our standard modules to your exact product requirements — from firmware to hardware.

How It Works

From requirements to mass production — our streamlined customization process.

1

Requirements Analysis

Share your product specifications, target certifications, and application requirements. Our engineers will recommend the right base module and customization scope.

2

Customization Design

Our engineering team configures firmware parameters, pinout assignments, antenna design, and mechanical specifications to match your product needs.

3

Sample & Testing

We ship custom samples within 2–3 weeks. Full functional test reports, RF test results, and certification pre-scan reports are included.

4

Mass Production

Scale up with consistent quality. MOQ as low as 100 units for custom modules. Full traceability and QC documentation provided.

Customization Options

What we can customize across our module lineup.

Firmware
Custom Firmware

Custom parameter configuration, feature additions, protocol modifications, and firmware flashing before shipment.

Hardware
Pinout & Peripherals

GPIO reassignment, peripheral remapping, voltage level changes, and custom pin headers.

RF
Antenna Design

Custom PCB antenna patterns, IPEX connector placement, or external antenna solutions for optimal RF performance.

Branding
Appearance & Packaging

Logo printing on the module shield, custom marking, and branded packaging for OEM products.

Certification
Pre-Certified Modules

All modules already carry FCC and CE type approval. We assist with additional market certifications as needed.

OEM/ODM
Full OEM/ODM Service

From custom module design to finished connected product. Small-batch OEM from 100 units, ODM available.
